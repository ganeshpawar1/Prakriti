PRAKRITI
A Green Initiative by MindSpark

ABOUT US

  • Started in 2022, Prakriti is a green initiative started by MindSpark which aims to promote environmental sustainability and conservation practices.
  • Through Prakriti we intend to raise awareness about the importance of preservation of natural resources.
  • We wish to encourage eco-friendly behaviors and lifestyles among individuals and communities around us.

PRAKRITI’22

  • Last year we inaugurated Prakriti with a session with Sagar-Mitra, an NGO focused on
    sustainability of resources.

  • It was an incredible opportunity for students of COEP to learn about the importance of
    utilization of natural resources and how awareness must be created.

  • Conducted plastic collection drives within COEP to recycle during MindSpark’22.

  • Generated a high volume of plastic, along with a general awareness among students generating a positive response.

PRAKRITI’23

  • This year we aim to amplify the scale of Prakriti, reaching a broader audience
    and making a larger positive impact on the environment.
  • Generating a bigger crowd allows us to create a bigger impact on sustainability.
  • As a tech-fest, it is our duty to help towards
    a more eco friendly lifestyle for everyone around us.

  • Cleanliness drives on local help with the help of NGO’s
